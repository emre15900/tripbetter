    Refund Policy for Trip Better to Africa

    Effective Date: Jan 2024

    1. Overview

    This Refund Policy applies to all purchases made through the trip better to Africa website ("Website"). By making a purchase, you agree to this Refund Policy.

    2. Cancellation and Refund Eligibility

    a. Travel Packages: Refund eligibility for travel packages is subject to the terms and conditions specified in the individual travel package details.

    b. Booking Fees: Some travel packages may include non-refundable booking fees. The non-refundable nature of these fees will be clearly communicated at the time of purchase.

    3. Cancellation by User

    a. Voluntary Cancellation: If you decide to voluntarily cancel your reservation or travel package, the refund eligibility will be in accordance with the terms and conditions specified at the time of booking.

    b. Refund Processing Time: Refunds, when applicable, will be processed within 3 business days from the date of cancellation. The refund will be issued in the original form of payment.

    4. Cancellation by Trip better to Africa

    a. Cancellation of Travel Package: In the event that Trip better to Africa cancels a travel package, users will be notified as soon as possible. Refund details will be communicated based on the circumstances of the cancellation.

    5. Unused Services

    a. No Refund for Unused Services: Refunds will not be provided for any portion of the travel package or services that you choose not to use.

    6. Refund Requests

    a. How to Request a Refund: To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at email provided on our website.Refund requests must be submitted within [3] days from the date of cancellation or as specified in the travel package details.

    b. Required Information: When requesting a refund, please provide your name, booking reference, and a detailed explanation of the reason for the refund.

    7. Changes to Refund Policy

    a. Policy Modifications: Trip better to Africa reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on the Website. Users are encouraged to review this Refund Policy periodically for any updates.

    8. Contact Information

    If you have any questions or concerns about this Refund Policy, please contact us at the email on the website.

    By making a purchase on the Website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Refund Policy.
