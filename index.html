    Help Children in Africa

    Swisderm, a dedicated tour and travel company, is committed to making a positive impact beyond travel arrangements by extending a helping hand to children in need. Operating in Africa for over three years, Swisderm has witnessed the challenges faced by hundreds of children and families affected by conflict, climate change, and now, the added burden of COVID-19.

    In regions like South Sudan and the Sahel, prolonged conflicts have disrupted families' access to food for an extended period. Meanwhile, East and Southern Africa grapple with the harsh consequences of climate change, leading to more frequent and severe droughts. Presently, Somalia, Ethiopia, and Kenya are grappling with their third severe drought episode in a decade, while West Africa is experiencing its worst food crisis in ten years, affecting 27 million people unsure of where their next meal will come from.

    Swisderm recognizes the urgent needs of vulnerable African children and families and is actively working to enhance access to healthcare and education. Our efforts also extend to protecting children from the adverse effects of conflict, such as child trafficking, forced early marriage, and child labor.

    However, none of this life-saving work is possible without your support. Your contribution will make a significant difference in our mission to provide assistance to those in desperate need. Together, we can create a positive impact on the lives of countless children and families across Africa.
